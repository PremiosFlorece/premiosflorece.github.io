About

Premios Florece was born in 2020 as an initiative of Jean Manzano, who, at the time, was working as director of a medical cannabis quality control laboratory. With the intention to celebrate the most prominent brands in the industry in a transparent way, allowing patients themselves to vote for their favorite products and companies. Manzano, together with a team of workers and "influencers" from the industry, carried out the first edition virtually.
