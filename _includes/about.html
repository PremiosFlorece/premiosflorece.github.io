About

Premios Florece is all about celebrating the best in the cannabis industry. Every year, we run a poll where the cannabis community votes for their favorites in categories like dispensaries, growers, manufacturers, and brands.

Our mission is to recognize and honor those who are making a real impact in the cannabis community. We believe in transparency and community involvement, so we let the people decide the winners through their s.

We want to be the go-to authority for recognizing excellence in the cannabis industry. Our vision is to inspire others to strive for greatness and contribute to the advancement of the cannabis industry.

Join us on this exciting journey to celebrate the best in the cannabis industry through Premios Florece. Together, we can shape the future of cannabis and contribute to its success!
